eBay Photo Tool v1.5

1. Open your GitHub repository: gooey31/-ebay-photo-tool
2. Replace app.py and requirements.txt with the files from this ZIP.
3. You may also upload README.md.
4. Commit the changes to the main branch.
5. Wait for Streamlit to rebuild, then refresh the app.
6. Test the three representative headrest photos first.

Important: the first launch may be slow while the new quality model downloads.
